Our goal is to connect you to a huge variety of cuisines so you can find and order any meal you desire, all through the Sizzler website.

You can easily find nearby restaurants by entering your Location, Area, or Pin Code in the search bar on the homepage. Alternatively, click the 'Location' icon to automatically share your current location and get an instant list of restaurants that deliver to you.

After placing your order, navigate to the 'My Orders' section to view real-time live tracking. You will see updates on when the food is being prepared, picked up by the delivery rider, and its estimated time of arrival (ETA). You will also receive updates via SMS or push notifications.

You can cancel your order immediately after placing it (usually within the first 2-3 minutes). Once the restaurant begins preparing the food, modifications or cancellations are generally not possible. For urgent issues, please contact our Customer Support team right away.
